The Philippines is a place that is visited, inhabited and loved by many. It is known for fostering beautiful settings, delicious cuisine, and hospitable locals. There is so much to explore in the Philippines, but if you dont have an idea of where to go first here are a couple suggestions for places that you might wanna visit.

Boracay

  • A very popular location for both tourists and locals alike. It is known for its pearly white sand beaches, calm breeze and serene azure waters.

Bonifcacio Global City

  • A very modern and industrialized city. It is known for its metropolis look, many cafes and ideal architecture.

Baguio

  • Is a great chance of pace for those who want a hybrid of a provincial yet modern vibe. It is significantly colder here than any other place in the Philippines due to its high altitude.
